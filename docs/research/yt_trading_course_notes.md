3. **Multi-Timeframe Alignment** — MTF Momentum already uses H1 → M15 → M5 alignment

5. **Session Trading** — London Breakout strategy captures session-based moves

### What We Could Add:
1. **Head & Shoulders / Double Top detection** — chart pattern recognition
2. **Wick Rejection Entries** — enter on wick rejection at key levels (stronger than body close)
3. **Top-Down Bias** — weekly/daily bias before M15/M5 entry (enhance regime detection)
4. **Break-Even Stop Management** — move SL to break-even after partial profit
5. **Candlestick Pattern Scoring** — weight signals higher when backed by strong candle patterns

### Instructor's Key Principles:
- Trade the HIGHER timeframe direction only
- Wait for CONFIRMATION (don't predict)
- Risk management > strategy (most traders fail on execution, not strategy)
- Be patient — not every day has a trade
